About Nexus Automate

Nexus Automate was born during Samuel Mwangi's industrial attachment at Kenyan HR Solutions, where he witnessed firsthand the inefficiencies of manual workflows. This experience sparked the realization that many businesses faced similar challenges, leading to the foundation of Nexus Automate.

Our mission is to empower businesses of all sizes to eliminate repetitive work and unlock their team's potential for innovation, growth, and meaningful impact. We envision a future where African businesses compete globally not just on talent and creativity, but on operational efficiency and smart automation.
